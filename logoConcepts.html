№ 01 Monogram · The Hammock R

The Hammock R

A geometric monogram where the R's leg dissolves into a slung hammock — the literal idea of rest hiding inside the letterform. The dot on the sling is the client, weightless. Reads as a serious financial mark first, a wink second.

The tracking dot.

A serif wordmark in the spirit of a private bank or a serious editorial publication. The single rose dot above the final stroke is the "tracking" pulse — a recurring blip that can animate (heartbeat, payment received, schedule confirmed). Restraint is the loudest move; the italic e hides the acronym's first letter.
